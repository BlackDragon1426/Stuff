The Games Basic Controls

A:Jump
	A in air:Extend wings
	-A on ground:Roll straight. + Left stick choose the direction
	A while flying:Put wings away

B:Melee
	-B:Block Melee/ Close Range Weapon

X:crouch and prone
	when moving:To start sliding
	-X when sprinting:To dive into prone

Right Stick:Look
	Click:Look down sight/Zoom

Left Stick:Movement
	Click:Sprint

Right Trigger:Shoot Right weapon/ Swing Right Weapon

Right Bumper:Reload Right weapon

Left Trigger:Throw grande/Shoot Left weapon/ swing Left Weapon

Left Bumper:Switch grande/reload Left weapon

D-Pad Up:Action/Pick up object
	When Crouching:Pick pocket some one if close and looking at them

D-Pad Down:Holster/Sheath weapon... so This is disabled in Competitive multiplayer, Not open World.

D-Pad Right:Switch Right Weapon
	-D-Pad Right:Quick Switch Weapons / Gadets

D-Pad Left:Switch Left Weapon
	-D-Pad Left:Quick Switch Weapons / Gadets

Back:Shows Upgrade and stats and/or score to the game

Start:Shows stats, upgrade, inventory, Quest/Objective...
